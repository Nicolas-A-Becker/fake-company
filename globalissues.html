How Companies Can Make a Difference to Global Problems

Companies are receiving positive responses from taking the lead in realizing social values without just pursuing profits. In particular, the movement to actively solve global problems is something that consumers pay attention to.There are different ways for companies to make a difference to global problems

  • Further developing energy productivity, reusing materials, and guaranteeing that families access medical care administrations assist organizations to lessen costs, increment benefits, and build long-term value and maintainability.
  • Thoughtfully and purposefully identify and collaborate with NGO/nonprofit partners who bring expertise as well as credibility, relationships, and access to communities where the company seeks to engage.
  • Companies can increase the scale of the problem by linking their own business model to social problems and seeking solutions,making other companies/communities join to solve the problem. This company can make more profits by solving these social problems as more consumers/customers are interested in the company.

    • Why Does It Matter?


    Consumers: Consumers these days are very aware of the companies and how they deal with our environment, they would not like businesses that would be a harm to our environment. Businesses with good images would bring in new customers and is a way to a long lasting successful business.

    Investors: Socially responsible investors already account for $30 trillion, approximately 20% of the estimated value of global capital markets. Many people are interested in investing in a company that is socially responsible and a company that would make them a profit. Having to solve global problems brings in more trust from the investors.
