Business Communication Issues and Market Effectiveness

The corporate world has become more complex than ever with more stakeholders. It is not easy to meet the needs of all internal or external stakeholders. However, it is necessary to ensure business survival and success in the long run. Internal stakeholders are more important because they have a direct and direct impact on business efficiency. External stakeholders can never be satisfied until internal stakeholders are satisfied. Therefore, communication plays an important role in business efficiency. It increases employee satisfaction and motivates them.

Business Communications Issues:

  1. The use of inappropriate tool of communication
    • The timely use of appropriate communication tools is very important for internal communication. Using the proper tool for communication helps engage in more efficient communication among employees.
  2. Emails
    • A 2005 study found that although 50 percent of all online communication is misunderstood, senders believe that their message is being received clearly, where it becomes a problem with the receiver when there is a lack of tone/body language to give context.
    • One of the growing internal communication problems is missing important internal emails. It happens because employees, especially senior executives, receive huge amounts of emails.
  3. Inappropriate/Unclear Feedback
    • Feedback is the most important part of all communication processes. It helps the recipient understand the sender's actual intention behind the message. Complete communication in a comprehensive and meaningful way. Lack of feedback leads to misinterpretation of interactions. This creates a communication barrier that prevents employees from decrypting the true purpose behind the message. It is a place where work inefficiency and dissatisfaction explode.
